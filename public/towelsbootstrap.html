Best Febrics To Make Towels

1.Most bath towels are made of cotton, but cotton towels vary in their softness, absorbency, durability and color-fastness. Fabric type is the most important feature of a bath towel. Here are some of the most common fabrics:

2.Bamboo bath towels • can be 100% bamboo, but are more often a bamboo and cotton blend. Bamboo bath towels are lush, supple and color-fast. The bamboo plant is naturally anti-bacterial, grows heartily in a pest-free environment and makes a super absorbent fabric.

3.Egyptian cotton bath towels • made of the highest quality cotton and are one of the most popular types of bath towels. Egyptian cotton fibers are extra-long and highly absorbent, making Egyptian cotton bath towels the most exquisite way to end a shower or bath. Luxurious Egyptian cotton has a very long lifespan when properly cared for.

4.Microfiber bath towels • a fairly new type of towel. Microfiber is made by blending polyester and polyamide fibers (80/20 ratio is common). Microfiber grabs dust, dirt, dry skin, makeup and oil without chemicals.

5.Organic cotton bath towels • are woven from cotton untouched by unnatural fertilizers or pesticides. Certified organic cotton is grown and harvested to leave the smallest possible carbon footprint, and organic cotton bath towels are usually created under the same earth-friendly mantra.

6.Pima cotton bath towels • are grown from the same plant used for Egyptian cotton, only Pima cotton (and its brand name, Supima cotton) is grown in southwestern U.S. Like their Egyptian cousins, Pima cotton bath towels are known for being strong and thirsty.

7.Turkish cotton bath towels • are made of extra-long cotton fibers similar to Egyptian cotton towels. The cotton grown in the Aegean region is soft, fluffy and strong. Turkish cotton bath towels are absorbent ( but not as absorbent as Egyptian cotton ) so they are ideal for warm climates with excessive moisture. Turkish cotton bath towels are known as being thick and luxurious.

Towel Standard Sizes

Washcloths Measurements: 12'' x 12''
Hand Towels Measurements: 20'' x 30''
Bath Towels Measurements: 30'' x 56''
Bath Sheets Measurements: 40'' x 70''




Washclothes
Washcloths are the smallest bath towel option, and they are commonly used to wash and dry your face outside of the shower. They also work well to cleanse and exfoliate your body while you shower.

Hand Towels
As their name suggests, hand towels are intended for drying your hands. These towels remain outside of the shower, and they can also be used to dry your face, as well.
Bath Towels
Bath towels are the most commonly used towel for drying off after a bath or shower. They are ideal for drying both your body and hair, as their size makes them soft and absorbent, but not overly heavy.
Bath Sheets
Bath sheets are the largest towel choice available, and they are optimal for drying off and wrapping up in after a shower or bath. They work particularly well if you seek more coverage. For reference, our oversized bath sheets are 67% bigger than our bath towels.
